About the College

A Legacy of Knowledge & Service

Vasantrao Naik Mahavidyalaya, situated in CIDCO, Aurangabad, is a leading arts, science and commerce institution affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University.

Named after the legendary farmer-statesman Vasantrao Naik, the college upholds its founder's vision of empowering rural and underprivileged students through quality higher education.

With NAAC accreditation, a thriving research culture, vibrant student activities, and state-of-the-art facilities, the college continues to shape generations of thoughtful citizens and professionals.
